A Few Words About Us

X Production is one of the world's largest steel manufacturers and a leading American metal company. Today the company is the #1 provider of sub-contract fabrication services.

Our Mission

Quality work in fabrication of steel structures and timely completion are the main essence of our business policy. We also strive for the close cooperation with our clients.

Our Vision

We aim to be among the top recognized organizations that are involved in multidisciplinary fabrication and erection services in various countries across the world.

Company Values

Our company is based on integrity, trust, teamwork, and quality. These values help us remain the leaders of the metalworking industry for decades and offer great products.
