Our history

The beginning of Woodcraft and our first products

We started Woodcraft in 1980 as a small carpentry business offering woodworking & custom furniture manufacturing services to local residents.

National expansion and promotion of our services

In 1995, we achieved greater success that we could ever dream of, as we started offering our services nationally to businesses and individual clients all over the US.

Opening new offices in European countries

We started our globalization in XXI century with opening two new offices in Netherlands and Spain, where our carpentry services proved to be very called-for.

Recognition of sustainable woodworking solutions

Today we are recognized as the #1 woodworking company with full equipment line for wood processing, custom cabinetry manufacturing, and global supply chain.
